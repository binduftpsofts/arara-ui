Borders & Radius

ArarA provides an extensive set of utilities for styling element borders, from basic widths and colors to complex radii and focus rings. Elevate your UI with precision control over boundaries and outlines.

Border Width

Control the thickness of an element's border across all sides or individual edges.

1
border
2
border-2
4
border-4
8
border-8
HTML
<div class="border border-indigo-200">...</div>
<div class="border-2 border-indigo-300">...</div>
<div class="border-4 border-indigo-400">...</div>
<div class="border-8 border-indigo-500">...</div>

Pro Tip: Side-Specific Widths

You can target specific sides using border-t-{size}, border-r-{size}, border-b-{size}, and border-l-{size}. This is incredibly useful for creating dividers or tabs without extra HTML elements.

Side-specific Rounding

You can independently round specific sides or corners by appending directional letters: t, b, l, r.

rounded-t-2xl
rounded-t
rounded-b-2xl
rounded-b
rounded-l-2xl
rounded-l
rounded-r-2xl
rounded-r
HTML
<!-- Top and Bottom -->
<div class="rounded-t-2xl">...</div>
<div class="rounded-b-2xl">...</div>

<!-- Left and Right -->
<div class="rounded-l-2xl">...</div>
<div class="rounded-r-2xl">...</div>
